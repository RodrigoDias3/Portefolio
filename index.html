About Me

Welcome to my digital portfolio. My name is Rodrigo Dias, I'm 19 years old and originally from Lisbon. Currently, I am in the 2nd year of my degree in Computer Engineering. With an average of 16, I have constantly looked for challenges and opportunities to expand my academic and professional horizons.

Programming is a recent reality in my life.

During my high school, I chose a Philosophy subject instead of the Applications and IT subject, this offered me not only a deeper understanding of the fundamental issues of human existence, but also developed my critical thinking capacity and analysis. By delving into debates about ethics and logic I discovered a passion for intellectual challenges and solving complex problems. This experience sparked an interest in logic and structured reasoning in me, leading me to further explore the vast world of technology and computing. I then chose to pursue Computer Engineering in college, where I can not only apply these analytical and critical thinking skills, but also delve into the art of programming, transforming abstract concepts into concrete and innovative solutions to the challenges of the modern world.

Today I see programming as a world where the only limit is the human imagination, and with fewer or more challenges everything is possible.

In my free time I like to dive and explore the world of video games or hang out with friends and enjoy sunny afternoons. One of my passions is water polo, an activity that combines my passion for the water with the camaraderie and competitiveness of team sports.

This digital portfolio is a sample of my work, projects and achievements to date. I invite you to explore and discover more about me and my journey so far.
